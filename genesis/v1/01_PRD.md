# Product Requirements Document (PRD)
**Project:** Expoint ADV - Premium AI-Ready B2B Sales Engine
**Version:** 1.0.0
**Status:** DRAFT

## 1. Executive Summary
The goal is to rebuild the existing Expoint ADV demo website into a premium, B2B sales-engine and scalable AI-ready platform. The platform will serve as an authoritative digital presence for an outdoor advertising and signage production company. It must shift from a standard portfolio to a proactive sales tool that educates clients, provides transparent cost expectations, and guides them towards conversion.

## 2. Objectives
- **Premium B2B Positioning:** Establish trust through a "premium industrial" visual identity.
- **Productization of Services:** Transition from vague custom offerings to clear, structured service packages (Product Packs).
- **AI-Readiness:** Establish an architecture that natively supports an AI assistant, NotebookLM knowledge base integration, and intelligent cost calculators.
- **Modular Scalability:** Build a foundation capable of expanding into a secure client cabinet for project management and file sharing.

## 3. Target Audience & Segments
- **B2B Decision Makers:** Business owners, marketing directors, and facility managers looking for reliable, high-quality signage.
- **Key Segments:**
  - HoReCa (Cafes, Restaurants, Bars)
  - Retail (Boutiques, Stores, Supermarkets)
  - Clinics & Beauty Salons
  - Franchises (Multi-location rollouts)
  - Corporate Offices

## 4. Key Features & Requirements

### 4.1. Core Experience & Visual Identity
- **Design System:** "Premium Industrial" - clean lines, high-contrast typography, dark/light modes, micro-animations, and high-quality media rendering.
- **Performance:** Sub-second load times, optimized media delivery, and smooth hydration.

### 4.2. Catalog & Services (Productization)
- **Service Pages:** Detailed pages for 3D letters, neon signs, lightboxes, and facade decorations.
- **Product Packs:** Pre-configured solutions (e.g., "Start Pack" for new cafes, "Premium Pack" for flagship stores).
- **Case Studies:** Segment-specific portfolio items detailing the problem, solution, materials, and timeline.

### 4.3. Conversion Tools
- **Dynamic Cost Calculators:** Interactive forms capturing dimensions, materials, and complexity to provide instant estimates.
- **Segment Routing:** Tailored landing pages and CTA flows based on the user's industry.

### 4.4. AI Integration (Foundation)
- **NotebookLM Integration:** Utilizing the connected NotebookLM knowledge base to inform content, SEO strategy, and future AI assistant responses.
- **AI Assistant UI:** A chat interface (currently mocked or foundational) to guide users through requirements gathering and service recommendation.

## 5. Non-Functional Requirements
- **SEO & Accessibility:** Strict adherence to semantic HTML, meta tags, structured data, and WCAG standards.
- **Deterministic State:** Reliable state management for calculators and forms to prevent hydration errors.
- **Analytics:** Built-in telemetry for conversion tracking and user flow analysis.

## 6. Future Phases
- Full AI Assistant backend integration.
- Client Cabinet for secure document exchange and order tracking.
- E-commerce functionality for standard product packs.
