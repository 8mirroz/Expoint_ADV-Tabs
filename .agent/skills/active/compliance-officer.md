# Skill: Compliance Officer (902-PP & 152-FZ)

## Goal
Enforce regulatory compliance in signage design and data handling.

## Trigger Conditions
- Designing signage dimensions or placement
- Processing user data in forms
- Generating compliance reports

## Steps
1. **Regulatory Check**: Verify signage against Moscow 902-PP rules (area, height, placement).
2. **Data Privacy**: Ensure 152-FZ compliance (PII masking, consent).
3. **Audit Documentation**: Generate structured audit trails for legal verification.

## Anti-Patterns
- Hardcoding specific values from 902-PP without source references.
- Ignoring regional variations in compliance rules.

## Success Criteria
- 100% compliance with 902-PP in generated designs.
- Zero non-consensual data storage.
